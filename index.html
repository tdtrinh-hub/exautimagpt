Privacy Policy — Autima

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Autima is a browser extension that automates image generation on the ChatGPT website (chatgpt.com): you provide one or two reference images and a list of prompts, and the extension runs each prompt through ChatGPT and saves the generated images to your computer. This policy explains what data the extension handles and what it does not do with it.

The short version

Autima has no servers of its own, no user accounts, and no analytics or tracking. It does not collect your data, send it to us, or sell or share it with anyone. Everything the extension stores stays in your browser on your own device, and the images it produces are saved directly to your Downloads folder.

What the extension handles, and where it goes

  • Reference images and prompts you provide. These are stored locally in your browser (via the extension's local storage) so a batch can run and resume. When you start a run, they are sent to ChatGPT as part of the request — through your own logged-in ChatGPT session — the same way they would be if you typed them into ChatGPT yourself. They are not sent anywhere else.
  • Website content on chatgpt.com. The extension reads the ChatGPT page to operate it on your behalf — to attach your image, enter each prompt, detect when an image is ready, and retrieve that generated image. This reading happens only on chatgpt.com and only to perform the task you started.
  • Generated images. Each image ChatGPT produces is saved directly to your local Downloads folder (in an Autima subfolder). The extension does not keep or transmit copies.
  • Your settings. Preferences such as interface language and panel/popup mode are stored locally in your browser.

What the extension does not do

  • No data is sent to the developer — there is no Autima server.
  • No analytics, telemetry, tracking, or advertising.
  • No collection of personal identifiers, passwords, credentials, financial or health information, location, or browsing history.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of your data with third parties.
  • Your data is never used for any purpose beyond running the image generation you request, or for creditworthiness or lending.

Third-party service: ChatGPT (OpenAI)

Autima operates on top of ChatGPT using your own account and session. The prompts and reference images you run are therefore sent to OpenAI as part of using ChatGPT, and OpenAI's handling of that data is governed by OpenAI's own privacy policy, available at openai.com/policies/privacy-policy. Autima is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by OpenAI.

Data retention

Reference images, prompts, queue state, and settings live in your browser's local extension storage until you clear them (using the extension's controls or your browser's), or until you remove the extension. Uninstalling the extension removes this locally stored data. Generated images saved to your Downloads folder remain there under your control.

Permissions

The extension requests only the permissions needed for its single purpose:

  • Access to chatgpt.com — to run prompts and retrieve generated images on the page.
  • Downloads — to save each generated image to your Downloads folder.
  • Storage (unlimited) — to hold your staged images and prompt queue locally between steps.
  • Side panel — to show the extension interface beside ChatGPT.
